Privacy Policy
Committed to protecting your information

Effective Date: 23rd September, 2024

Money-Tour is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect
When you use our Money-Tour web application, we may collect the following types of information:
  • Personal Information: This includes information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, username, and other account details.
  • Transaction Data: Information related to your financial transactions, including type, amount, and date of transactions.
  • Usage Data: Data about how you use our app, such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and the time and date of your visits.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
  • To provide and maintain our service: We use your personal and transaction data to manage your account and provide you with the features of our application.
  • To improve our services: Usage data helps us understand how users interact with our web app, enabling us to enhance user experience.
  • To communicate with you: We may send you updates, notifications, or respond to your inquiries.
3. How We Protect Your Information
We are committed to ensuring the security of your personal information. We use industry-standard security measures, such as encryption and secure servers, to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.
4. Third Party Services
We may use third-party services (such as payment processors, hosting providers, etc.) to support our application. These third-party providers have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their actions or practices.
5. Cookies
Our application may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our app.
6. Data Retention
We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. We may also retain your data to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.
7. Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
  • Access and Correction: You can request access to your data and correct any inaccuracies.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to any legal obligations we have.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of certain communications or features, such as marketing emails.
8. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page, with the updated effective date.
9. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@moneytour.com.
