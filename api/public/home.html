About Money-Tour
Money-Tour is a user-friendly web application designed to help individuals monitor their personal finances and manage their expenses with ease. Whether you're looking to monitor your spending habits, plan a budget, or simply get a better handle on where your money is going, Money-Tour provides the tools you need to stay on top of your finances.
Key Features
Expense Tracking: Easily log and categorize your daily transactions, from bills and groceries to entertainment and travel.
Income Monitoring: Keep a record of your income sources and track your monthly cash flow.
Real-Time Balance Updates: Instantly view your current balance to know how much money you have available at any given time.
Transaction History: Access a complete list of your past transactions, helping you review your spending patterns over time.
User-Friendly Interface: Our clean and intuitive design ensures that managing your finances is both simple and efficient.
